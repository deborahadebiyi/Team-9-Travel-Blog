About Us

Who are we?

Hi, we are Home Away! This travel blog is run by Deborah, Joanna and Dorcas, three travel enthusiasts who want to share our journeys with everyone. We created this blog to satisfy all your travel needs by giving you an inside look into our experiences. We provide you with lots of tips, tricks and general information on our travels around the world. From top destinations to festivals and attractions, we will cover all the essentials to ensure you have your own amazing trip!

To stay updated on all our latest travels, connect with us on social media! We would love to see where you have been and interact with you.

Competitions

Occasionally, we partner with some amazing companies to host giveaways. To give you a taste of what's in store, some companies we have collaborated with in the past include EasyJet, American Tourister and Airbnb. We always ensure to maintain equity in our competitions, so we regularly check for any misconduct. Anyone found to abuse the rules will be disqualified from future competitions.

Please keep an eye out for more details on upcoming competitions by checking our competition page or subscribe to our newsletter so you will never miss anything again!
